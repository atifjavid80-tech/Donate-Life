Why Donate Blood?

Every blood donation can save up to three lives. Donating blood helps hospitals maintain life-saving supplies for emergencies, surgeries, and cancer treatments.

  • It reduces harmful iron stores in your body.
  • It helps those in need during emergencies.
  • It’s a simple act that can change lives.

After Donation — What to Do

Here are some helpful tips to recover quickly after donating:

  • Drink plenty of water and juice.
  • Eat foods rich in iron such as spinach, beans, and red meat.
  • Avoid heavy exercise for at least 24 hours.
